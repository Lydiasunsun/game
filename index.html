Hunt The Lecturers

Introduction

General Rules

  1. Zombie Queen is hungry, she want to eat(collapse) lecturers.
  2. Each lecturer eaten means score +1.
  3. Zombie Queen won't leave the canvas.
  4. Zombie Queen can't pass obstacles, but lecturers can.
  5. Step in the well with a random location will cause death. (There is one well with a fixed location, which is safe, and if you win then its safe to step on any well).
  6. Stepping on the water will slow down the player.
  7. Read the clues. They might be helpful.
  8. Enjoy the music.(Hit play in level 1 to start the game and music.)
  9. You can play the game without login or register.

Level 1

  1. Have to eat at least 4 lecturers before time runs out to pass level 1.
  2. After time finish, if you ate at least 4 lecturers, jump to the castle and hit enter.

Level 2

  1. Eat all lectures on the canvas in a limited time to pass level 2.
  2. Do not eat the bird. We all love animals. Otherwise, you die.
  3. There are three treasure boxes at random locations with random colors. They can be opened.
  4. The food box will allow a chicken leg to show up for 8 seconds; a chicken leg may attract lectures to come closer for 8 seconds. You can stay near the chicken leg to get lecturers.
  5. Time box will give you extra 10 seconds.
  6. There may be empty box and fake decorations.
